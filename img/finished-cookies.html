Five Star Chocolate Chip Cookies

Chocolate chip cookies from a winning recipe. Bake up a big batch of these classic chocolate chip cookies.

48
cookies

Ingredients

1 1/2 cups Land O Lakes® Butter, softened

1 1/4 cups sugar

1 1/4 cups firmly packed brown sugar

2 large Land O Lakes® Eggs

1 tablespoon vanilla extract

3 3/4 cups all-purpose flour

1 teaspoon baking powder

1 teaspoon baking soda

1 teaspoon salt

1 (12-ounce) package (2 cups) semi-sweet chocolate chips

How to make

  1. STEP 1

    Heat oven to 375°F.

  2. STEP 2

    Combine butter, sugar, brown sugar, eggs and vanilla in bowl. Beat at medium speed until creamy. Add flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt; beat at low speed until well mixed. Stir in chocolate chips.

  3. STEP 3

    Drop dough by rounded tablespoonfuls, 2 inches apart, onto ungreased cookie sheets. Bake 10-12 minutes or until light golden brown. Cool 2 minutes on cookie sheet; remove to cooling rack.

Read the Story Behind the Recipe

Pecan Toffee Chocolate Chip Cookies

Stir in 1 cup chopped pecans and 1/2 cup English toffee bits with chocolate chips.

Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Cookies

Omit 1 cup flour. Substitute 1 cup uncooked quick-cooking oats.

Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies

Omit 1 cup flour. Substitute 1 cup peanut butter.

Five Star Chocolate Chip Bars

Prepare cookie dough as directed. Spread into lightly greased 15x10x1-inch baking pan. Bake 25-30 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. Cool completely. Cut into 48 bars.
