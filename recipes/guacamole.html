Guacamole

Description

A short and easy recipe to prepare traditional mexican Guacamole.

Ingredients

  • ½ kg Avocados, peeled and whithout pit.
  • 1 Tomato chopped.
  • ½ Small onion chopped.
  • ½ Cup of finely chopped cilantro.
  • Jucie of 2 limes.
  • Salt and pepper to liking.
  • 1 Small green chile, chopped (Optional).

Steps

  1. Mash the avocado flesh until soft
  2. Add tomato, onion, chile and cilantro, mix until well incorporated.
  3. Add lime juice, salt and pepper, then mix thoroughly.
  4. Serve along with totopos or freshly made tortillas.
