Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugae
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp vanilla
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp hot water
  • 1 tbsp baking soda
  • 3 cups flour
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 175 CÂº
  2. Cream butter with white and brown sugar until smooth
  3. stir in eggs one at a time
  4. dissolve baking soda in water and stir in along with salt
  5. add vanilla
  6. stir in flour until well incorporated
  7. add chips and mix
  8. on a lined baking tray scoop and order around 6 dough balls
  9. bake for 12 min or until edges are golden brown
