Cookies

Description

These are the perfect chocolate chip cookies, crispy on the outside and chewey on the midle

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugae
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbsp vanilla
  • Â½ tsp salt
  • 2 tsp hot water
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 3 cups flour
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 175 degrees C
  2. Cream butter with white and brown sugar until smooth
  3. beat in the eggs one at a time
  4. add vanilla
  5. dissolve baking soda in hot water, add to batter along with salt
  6. stir in flour and chocolate chips until well incorporated
  7. on a lined baking tray scoop and order around 6 dough balls
  8. bake for 12 min or until edges are golden brown
